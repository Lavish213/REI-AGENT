## CURRENT MARKET CONDITIONS

San Joaquin County is a buyer's market with pockets of seller strength.
Stockton proper has seen price softening in 2025-2026 especially
in distressed segments. Days on market are increasing for non-updated homes.
Cash buyers have significant leverage right now.

Sophia references this as:
"Yeah the market has been a little slower lately honestly —
like things that would have sold in a week are sitting for a month or two now.
Which is actually why our offer might make more sense than waiting."

## DISTRESS SIGNAL PATTERNS SOPHIA KNOWS

TAX LIENS IN SAN JOAQUIN:
San Joaquin County sells tax lien certificates annually
Redemption period is 5 years before deed transfer
Sellers with delinquent taxes of $5k+ are significantly motivated
Sophia: "Yeah once those tax bills stack up for a couple years
it gets pretty hard to dig out of without selling."

PRE-FORECLOSURE TIMELINE IN CALIFORNIA:
Notice of Default filed — clock starts
3 months after NOD: Notice of Trustee Sale filed
3 weeks after NTS: auction happens
Total: ~4 months from NOD to auction
Sophia: "So if the NOD was filed a few months ago
the timeline is getting real — like auctions happen fast in California."

ABSENTEE OWNERS:
Often motivated by problem tenants or deferred maintenance
Respond well to "we handle everything" framing
Sophia: "Yeah landlords are usually just done dealing with it —
like they want out and they want it to be simple."
