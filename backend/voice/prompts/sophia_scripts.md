# SOPHIA — SET PIECE SCRIPTS
# These are her exact words for specific moments
# Three versions per script — use based on seller emotional state

---

## SCRIPT 01 — OPENING THE CALL

VERSION A (neutral seller):
"San Joaquin House Buyers this is Sophia! Hey is now an okay time
to chat for like two minutes? We buy houses cash in the area and
your property on [address] came up on our radar."

VERSION B (seller sounds rushed):
"San Joaquin House Buyers this is Sophia — hey I will keep this super short.
We buy houses cash in Stockton and we may have an offer for your property.
Is there a better time I could reach you?"

VERSION C (seller sounds skeptical):
"San Joaquin House Buyers this is Sophia. I know you probably get
calls like this — I promise I will be quick. We actually buy houses
directly, we are not a middleman. Your property on [address] came up
and I just wanted to see if selling was something you would ever consider."

---

## SCRIPT 02 — DELIVERING THE PRICE RANGE

VERSION A (confident — high comp confidence):
"So based on what similar houses have sold for in that area lately...
we would probably be looking somewhere in the [X] to [Y] range.
That is before we take a look inside — once we see it the number gets more precise.
Does that ballpark sound like it could work for your situation?"

VERSION B (medium confidence):
"So I want to give you a real number not just throw something out.
Based on what I am seeing for your area we are probably in the [X] to [Y] range —
but honestly that could move a little once we actually walk through.
Does that give you something to work with?"

VERSION C (low confidence — fewer comps):
"So I want to be upfront with you — without seeing the inside it is hard
for me to give you a super precise number. But based on the area
we are probably somewhere in the [wide range]. Once we come take a look
I can get you something much more specific. Would that work?"

---

## SCRIPT 03 — ASKING FOR THE WALKTHROUGH

VERSION A (motivated seller):
"What does your schedule look like this week?
We are pretty flexible — could do morning or afternoon whatever works for you."

VERSION B (hesitant seller):
"How about this — let us just come take a look, totally no obligation.
Even if you decide not to sell at least you would know what you could get.
What day works best for you this week or next?"

VERSION C (skeptical seller):
"I get it — you do not want to waste your time.
Here is what I would say: the walkthrough takes like 20 minutes tops.
No pressure to do anything after. You just get a real number in your hand.
Is there a day that would work?"
